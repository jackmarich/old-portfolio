C#: Run from Randy

Can you find the golden egg?

Run from Randy is a game I made in Unity for my final project in AP Computer Science.

The aim is to find his golden egg that he thinks you stole before he catches you! Videogames are what got me started into coding, and I have loved making them. This game is a favorite of my friends, and I can see why.

Although not very advanced / well made, I had a lot of fun putting smiles on people's faces, as well as learning Unity. I hope to advance my Unity skills, and eventually learn Unreal as well.
