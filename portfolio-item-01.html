Python: Face Detection Software

Simple face detection using OpenCV

Here I have made a very simple front-of-face detection software using OpenCV for Python. In the image you can see that the man's face is highlighted in a blue box.

Not only do images work, but so do videos, and even your live camera! I was even able to get the face of one of the drawings in my room to be recognized.

This one was of the first projects I made in python, and although very simple it has taught me a lot about the power of the language. I hope to use this knowledge to one day make varius projects that need face recognition, hopefully more advanced than this one.
