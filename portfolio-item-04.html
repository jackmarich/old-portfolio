Java: henri Helps

Making minecraft more fun

Henri Helps was a project made my two friends and I during my school district's hackathon. It even ended up winning one of the competitions, which was pretty neat as it was my first ever hackathon that I have attended. I hope to do more!

This project uses cho:here's NLP model to provide answers for students who are in troubling times. You ask it something that you are struggling with, and it gives you a quick answer to help alleviate your stress.
